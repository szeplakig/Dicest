Tutorial
  • D(sides): Throws a dice with N sides
  • explode(dice): If the dice inside throws max, then it is thrown again.
  • multiple(count, dice): Throws the inside dice N times and sums them up.
  • largest(keep, ...dices): Keeps and sums the N best dice throws.
  • They are nestable inside each other.
  • largest(1, D(10), multiple(4, D(4)))
